<h3>The Summary ABox</h3>
<ul>
<li>Map ABox $A$ to $A'$ for scalability using $f$
<br>
$\begin{array}{l}
C(a) \in A \implies C(f(a)) \in A'\\
R(a,b) \in A \implies R(f(a), f(b)) \in A'
\end{array}$</li>
<li> We choose <em>concept sets</em> as f</li>
</ul>
</section>
